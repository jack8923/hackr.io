Help & FAQs
Can we rely on your tutorials and trainings in terms of their quality?
To answer this, we would like to say that it’s our business to provide quality tutorials without charging a fee. We are striving hard to maintain the quality of our tutorials as high as possible. Our tutorials and training materials are being used by a lot of freelancer trainers and training companies as well to teach their students.
What is the mission of hackr.io?
Our mission is to deliver Simply Easy Learning with clear, crisp, and to-the-point content on a wide range of technical and non-technical subjects without any preconditions and impediments.
How can I help hackr.io?
It will be a great help to us if you can point out any typing errors, spelling mistakes or incorrect grammar used in any of the tutorials. Your contribution is extremely valuable and it will certainly help bring further improvement in our service.
I don't want to use my Google account to sign-in. What should I do?
We started out with the idea that using a Google account to sign-in would be the simplest and fastest way to login to Hackr, but we realized on the way that it's not something that everyone is comfortable with. We'll be introducing more signup methods pretty soon.
